About La Bouquiniste Américaine

If your're looking for how I made this app and its models, you can read about it on my blog here.

What is it?

La Bouquiniste Américaine is a book recommendation system that I made for those who love to read and know what they like. All the books listed on this website, either in French or in English, should be available for free through Project Gutenberg for English texts or Bibebook for French texts.

Raison d'Etre

I made this app because I love languages, and hope that people can use this tool to improve their second language skills. If people use it to find books in their first language as well, that's just another benefit. I would love to add more languages for people to use, so if you're familiar with any websites of free books in another language, please let me know! You can find out how to reach me on my contact page. You can also contact me about any errors you find.
